How does Pedal work and how does it

help against theft?

Register your bike through Pedal to make it an official registration linked with the LAPD

Keep an eye out for your mail to recieve our special QR code stickers that will be placed on the bike

If your bike is stolen you can report it on the website and any bike with the QR code can be identified if found by the police and returned

48% of bike are recovered by Police but only 5% of those bikes are brought back to the owners. There has been over 210,905 bike thiefts, 2% of the thiefts are arrested, and few as 70% of thefts are even reported.

Register your bike with Pedal to make it an official LAPD registration. This will increases the probability of your bike being returned if stolen.
